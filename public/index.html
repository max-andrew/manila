Confidential USDC payroll, paid daily by an agent.

Under 1% of payroll runs on stablecoins — a public chain turns every salary into a permanent public record. Manila gives an agent two USDC rails, run from plain English under policy controls no instruction can talk past:

  • Sealed daily salary — private transfers paid every day; gas-free nanopayments make per-day payroll cost a fraction of a cent, impractical on ACH or wire.
  • Programmable equity — RSU grants priced by a live stock oracle and settled in USDC on-chain, the agent releasing each tranche as it vests.

Equity vesting — RSUs settled in USDC

Equity that pays in cash. Each grant vests a number of RSU shares on a cliff; on release the contract reads a live company share price from an on-chain oracle and pays the vested shares' value in USDC — so the grant tracks the real stock, but settles in stablecoin. The agent releases it, signed by the Dynamic wallet; every release is verifiable on Arc.
